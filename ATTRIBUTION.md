## 2. Hindi and English Translations + Word-by-Word Meanings

**Source:** ramayana.info
**URL:** https://ramayana.info
**Nature of use:** Live fetch per shloka; content is NOT embedded in the app

Translations are fetched from ramayana.info at read time and cached locally
on the user's device for offline access. The content is not redistributed
or mirrored. This app links to ramayana.info as a live source.
